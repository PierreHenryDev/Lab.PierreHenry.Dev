In *The Almanack of Naval Ravikant*, Eric Jorgenson distills Naval's wisdom on leveraging skills, making sound judgments, and thinking long-term to achieve wealth and freedom. This weekly action plan will help you integrate these insights into your daily routine, ensuring consistent progress towards your goals.

## Key ideas to apply this week

- Leverage your skills and time to multiply efforts.
- Develop sound judgement to make better decisions.
- Adopt a long-term mindset for sustainable success.

## Weekly action plan

- **Monday**: Leverage Skills — Identify your top three skills and brainstorm how to apply them more effectively in your work.
- **Tuesday**: Judgement Reflection — Reflect on a recent decision. Write down what you learned and how you can improve future decisions.
- **Wednesday**: Long-Term Thinking — Review your long-term goals. Adjust them if necessary based on your current priorities.
- **Thursday**: Skill Application — Choose one of your identified skills and find a small project or task to apply it this week.
- **Friday**: Weekly Review — Review the actions you took this week. Note any successes and areas for improvement.

By dedicating just a few minutes each day to these actions, you can effectively integrate the principles from *The Almanack of Naval Ravikant* into your life. Consistency is key; let each small step bring you closer to your goals of wealth and freedom.
