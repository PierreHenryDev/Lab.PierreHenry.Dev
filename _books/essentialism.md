In today's fast-paced tech environment, it's easy to become overwhelmed by commitments that do not align with your goals. This weekly action plan will help you implement the principles of Essentialism, allowing you to focus on what truly matters and create more space for your personal and professional growth.

## Key ideas to apply this week

- Prioritise high-value tasks over low-value commitments.
- Learn to say no to distractions and unnecessary obligations.
- Create a routine that fosters focus and intentionality.

## Weekly action plan

- **Monday**: Commitment Review — Review your current commitments and identify at least two to eliminate.
- **Tuesday**: Saying No — Use your response template to decline at least one low-priority request.
- **Wednesday**: Weekly Focus — Spend 20 minutes outlining the steps needed to progress on your chosen project.
- **Thursday**: Time Audit — Track your time for the day and identify any distractions that hinder your focus.
- **Friday**: Reflect and Adjust — Reflect on the week. What worked? What did not? Adjust your commitments for the next week.

By systematically applying these principles from Essentialism, you can reclaim your time and focus on what truly matters in your career and personal life. Remember, it's not about doing more; it's about doing what counts.
