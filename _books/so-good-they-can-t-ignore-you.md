Cal Newport's 'So Good They Can’t Ignore You' challenges the conventional wisdom of following your passion. Instead, it emphasises the importance of developing rare and valuable skills that can lead to greater career satisfaction and financial success. This action plan is designed specifically for software engineers and tech professionals aiming to enhance their career trajectories while pursuing financial independence.

## Key ideas to apply this week

- Focus on skill development over passion.
- Mastery leads to career leverage.
- Deliberate practice is essential for skill acquisition.

## Weekly action plan

- **Monday**: Skill Assessment — Complete the list of rare skills and assess proficiency.
- **Tuesday**: Deliberate Practice Setup — Define your practice goal and gather necessary resources.
- **Wednesday**: Engage with Learning Material — Spend 20 minutes on a course or tutorial related to your chosen skill.
- **Thursday**: Practice Session — Dedicate 30 minutes to deliberate practice on the skill you are developing.
- **Friday**: Networking — Connect with the expert you identified and have a brief conversation.

By following this action plan, you will take concrete steps toward mastering rare skills that can significantly enhance your career leverage. Remember, consistent application of these strategies is key to long-term success.
