In 'The Millionaire Next Door', Thomas J. Stanley and William D. Danko reveal that many millionaires live below their means and prioritise financial discipline. This weekly action plan is designed for software engineers and tech professionals aiming for financial independence. By dedicating just a few minutes each day, you can cultivate habits that lead to sustainable wealth.

## Key ideas to apply this week

- Wealth is often built through consistent behaviours rather than high income.
- Living below your means is a key trait of millionaires.
- Discipline in spending and saving leads to long-term financial success.

## Weekly action plan

- **Monday**: Spending Awareness — Review last month's spending categories and identify any patterns.
- **Tuesday**: Savings Commitment — Set up an automatic transfer to your savings account based on your savings goal.
- **Wednesday**: Lifestyle Assessment — Choose one discretionary expense to reduce or eliminate this week.
- **Thursday**: Networking — Connect with a financially savvy colleague or mentor to discuss wealth-building strategies.
- **Friday**: Reflect and Adjust — Reflect on the week’s actions and adjust your strategies for the next week.

By consistently applying these actions, you can develop the habits necessary for building wealth. Remember, small changes can lead to significant results over time. Stay disciplined, and your success is closer than you think.
